Introduction

This activity is purely educational, aimed at raising HITCON attendees' awareness of WiFi security in cybersecurity. We will display the credentials entered by attendees on the fishing WiFi on the dashboard and will delete the data without keeping any backups after the conclusion of HITCON CMT 2023.


Propagandize

1. Avoid using untrusted WiFi networks.
2. Verify the URL and certificates to identify phishing websites.
3. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) to enhance account security.
